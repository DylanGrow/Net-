# 🌐 CompTIA Network+ (V9) Intensive Course Syllabus

> **Course Objective:** Master the foundational networking, security, and troubleshooting skills required to pass the **CompTIA Network+ (V9)** certification exam through intensive **5-hour practical sessions**.

---

## 📅 Course Overview

| Week | Theme | Core Exam Objectives | 🛠️ 5-Hour Practical Lab Session |
|:----:|:------|:---------------------|:--------------------------------|
| **1** | **Network Architecture** | • **OSI Model** Layers (1-7)<br>• **Cloud Concepts** (VPC, SaaS, IaaS, PaaS)<br>• **Ports & Protocols** (SSH, DNS, DHCP, SNMP) | Tracing a web server request through all seven OSI layers and analyzing which cloud deployment model (IaaS vs. PaaS) best fits an expanding e-commerce platform. |
| **2** | **Addressing & Physical Infrastructure** | • **IPv4 Addressing** (Subnetting, CIDR, VLSM)<br>• **Network Topologies** (Spine/leaf, mesh)<br>• **Transmission Media** (Fiber, copper) | Calculating a subnetting scheme for a three-tier network architecture using CIDR notation and selecting the appropriate fiber transceivers for a data center backbone. |
| **3** | **Routing & Switching Configuration** | • **Routing Technologies** (BGP, EIGRP, OSPF)<br>• **Route Selection & NAT**<br>• **Switching Tech** (VLANs, Spanning Tree, MTU) | Hands-on lab configuring VLANs on managed switches to separate corporate and guest traffic, followed by setting up OSPF routing between simulated branch offices. |
| **4** | **Wireless Design & Deployment** | • **Wireless Devices** (Channels, SSIDs, Encryption, Antennas)<br>• **Physical Installations** (Power, Environmental) | Conducting a mock wireless site survey for an office floorplan, selecting 802.11 channels to avoid overlapping interference, and calculating PoE budgets for the access points. |
| **5** | **Documentation & Lifecycle Management** | • **Documentation** (Logical/Physical Diagrams, IPAM, Rack Diagrams)<br>• **Change Management** (Request Tracking, Baselines) | Drafting comprehensive physical and logical network diagrams for a new server room and executing a formal change management request to upgrade core infrastructure. |
| **6** | **Monitoring & Disaster Recovery** | • **Disaster Recovery** (RPO, RTO, MTTR, Hot/Cold Sites)<br>• **Network Monitoring** (SNMP, Packet Capture)<br>• **Network Services** | Deploying SNMP to monitor network traffic for bottlenecks and designing a failover strategy to a hot site that meets a strict 1-hour Recovery Time Objective (RTO). |
| **7** | **Access Control & Logical Security** | • **Logical Security** (MFA, IAM, RADIUS, Least Privilege)<br>• **Physical Security** (Locks, Cameras)<br>• **Audits & Compliance** | Implementing Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to secure a remote desktop support environment and ensure compliance with data locality laws. |
| **8** | **Threat Mitigation & Network Defense** | • **Types of Attacks** (DoS, MAC Flooding, ARP/DNS Poisoning)<br>• **Network Segmentation** (IoT)<br>• **Defense** (Hardening, ACLs) | Simulating an ARP spoofing and MAC flooding attack in a sandboxed lab environment, then configuring Access Control Lists (ACLs) and device hardening techniques to block the exploit. |
| **9** | **Advanced Network Troubleshooting** | • **Troubleshooting Methodology** (Identify → Document)<br>• **Cabling/Hardware Issues**<br>• **Performance & Service Issues** | Working through a 5-hour simulated IT support ticket queue, utilizing command-line tools and protocol analyzers to diagnose latency, DHCP pool exhaustion, and misconfigured subnet masks. |

---

## 🚀 Learning Outcomes

By the end of this 9-week intensive program, students will be able to:
*   ✅ Design and implement secure, scalable network architectures.
*   ✅ Configure advanced routing and switching protocols in real-world scenarios.
*   ✅ Perform comprehensive wireless site surveys and deployments.
*   ✅ Execute disaster recovery plans and monitor network health effectively.
*   ✅ Mitigate modern cyber threats through segmentation and hardening.
*   ✅ Apply professional troubleshooting methodologies to resolve complex IT issues.
